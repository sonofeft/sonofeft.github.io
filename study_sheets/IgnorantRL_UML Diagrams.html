UML Diagrams
A class is represented as a box with 3 compartments
Class Name, Attributes and Methods
Types and Default Values can also be included
+ public, - private, # protected and ~ package
4 Types: Bidirectional, Unidirectional, Aggregation and Reflexive
Aggregation includes Composition Aggregation
Multiplicity is included as well: one to one, one to many, many to many, etc.
Child (subclass) points at Parent (super class) with an open arrow.
Child inherits all values and methods and can create unique values and methods.
Abstract Class
In diagram, both Point and Circle ReImplement the Abstract moving commands.
Note that Circle carries an Instance of Point
A One Way dependency... "Has A" or "Is Part Of"
e.g. Wallet has Money, but Money does not necessarily have Wallet
No Ownership or Parent/Child Relationship is implied
Car is NOT the exclusive container of Engine
Composition - Special form of Aggregation... Both are Required. Both classes have same life span.
Declare numbers (cardinality) between classes
House has 1 Kitchen, 1 Bath and 1 or more Bedroom objects... all of which inherit from Room
Has exactly 0 or 1 Mortgage
0..1, May be 1
1, Must be 1
1..*, Must be 1 or More
0..*, May be 1 or More
