Regularization
Not Trusting the Data Too Much
Overfitting causes Validation Loss to Rise at the End
Stop Earlier - hard in practice
Penalize Model Complexity... i.e. Regularization
min of Loss(Data | Model) + complexity( Model )
May give non-informative features more weight than a more complex model would
L2 regularization
Penalize Big Weights
(i.e. Assume weights should cluster near zero)
Linear Models prefer Flatter Slopes
Bayesian Prior weights centered around zero and normally distributed.
Needs to be tuned for problem
called Regularization Rate
When correct, training loss may be higher but test loss should be lower
